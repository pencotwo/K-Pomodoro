K-Pomodoro is a minimalist Pomodoro timer for Windows.

Stay focused with timed work sessions and short breaks, all
tracked through a clean, distraction-free interface.

Features:
- Configurable work cycles (2–8 sessions per day)
- 25-minute work sessions with 5-minute breaks
- Visual progress grid — see exactly where you are in your day
- Work log — review completed sessions with notes and next-session hints
- Dark, minimal UI that stays out of your way
- No ads, no accounts, no internet connection required

Perfect for students, developers, writers, and anyone who wants
to build a focused daily work habit.

繁體中文
================================================================

K-Pomodoro 是一款專為 Windows 設計的極簡番茄鐘工作計時器。

透過清晰無干擾的介面，幫助你維持專注，完成每日的工作循環。

功能特色：
- 自訂每日工作循環數（2 至 8 個循環）
- 每循環 25 分鐘工作、5 分鐘休息
- 視覺化進度列，一眼掌握今日完成進度
- 工作紀錄 — 記錄每次完成的工作內容與下次提示
- 深色極簡介面，專注工作不分心
- 無廣告、無帳號、無需網路連線

適合學生、工程師、寫作者，以及所有想建立高效工作習慣的人。
