Privacy Policy — K-Pomodoro

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Data Collection
K-Pomodoro does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app runs entirely on your local device.

Internet Access
K-Pomodoro does not access the internet. No data is sent to any server or third party.

Local Storage
The app may save settings (such as cycle count) locally on your device using Windows application storage. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services
K-Pomodoro does not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs.

Children's Privacy
K-Pomodoro does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy
Any future updates to this policy will be reflected in the app's Store listing.
